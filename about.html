STORY

Hi, my name is Lorden and I'm a 16-year-old multimedia design enthusiast. I dream of making high-quality design accessible to everyone.

I started my journey with a simple tablet when I was 12. I was fascinated by how I could use it to create digital art and graphics. I spent hours experimenting and learning new things.

When I was in Junior High, my mom got me a laptop. This was a turning point for me. With a laptop, I could now use more advanced software like Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator. I also had access to more online resources and tutorials.

Over the next few years, I honed my skills and developed my own unique style. I started taking on freelance projects and even created my own brand Grafix™ .

In the 9th grade, I met my best friend Fhaye. She's an amazing person who has inspired me in so many ways. She's the muse behind some of my best work.

I'm still learning and growing as a designer, but I'm proud of what I've accomplished so far. I'm passionate about using my skills to help others. I want to make high-quality design accessible to everyone, regardless of budget.

I believe that everyone has a creative spark inside them. I want to help others discover and nurture their creativity. I want to show them that they can create anything they set their minds to.

That's my story. I'm just a kid with a dream, but I'm determined to make it happen. I'm excited to see what the future holds for me and for the world of multimedia design.
